Why Go Hands-Free?

Real-Time Pitch & Speed

Pinch your thumb and index finger to warp audio instantly. Left hand controls pitch, right hand controls speed.

Performance Recording

Capture your sessions with synchronized audio and video. Perfect for DJs, musicians, and content creators.

AI-Powered Vision

Built with Apple's Vision Framework for ultra-low latency hand tracking and automatic calibration.

Your Hands are the Mixer

Forget tiny sliders and buttons. GestureFlow turns the air in front of you into a mixing console.

  • Touchless Interface

    Simply hold your hands up. The app auto-detects your presence.

  • Import Your Audio

    Load tracks from Files or your Video Library effortlessly.

  • Private & Secure

    No ads. No tracking. All processing happens on-device.
